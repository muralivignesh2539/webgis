About Thekkumbhagom

Chavara Thekkumbhagom, also known as Chavara South, is an island village located in the Kollam District of Kerala, India. It lies about 10 km from Kollam City and is surrounded by the serene Ashtamudi Lake, close to the coast of the Indian Ocean.

Panchayath Information System

This portal is your digital guide to the Thekkumbhagom Grama Panchayath. We have developed this comprehensive information system to promote transparency and enable data-driven planning for our community.

Here, you can explore interactive maps that show the core components of our Panchayath, including:

  • Public Services: Locations of all Schools and Hospitals.
  • Our Community: Detailed Population Distribution.
  • Economy & Recreation: Key Tourism and Leisure spots.
  • Our Land: The physical geography of our area, including Elevation (DEM).
  • Boundaries: The official Administrative Boundary of our Panchayath.

This tool helps us understand our community's needs, manage our resources effectively, and plan for sustainable development. We invite you to explore the map and learn more about our home.
