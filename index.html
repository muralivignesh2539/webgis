About Thekkumbhagom

Chavara Thekkumbhagom (or Chavara South) is an island village in Kollam District, Kerala, located about 10 km from Kollam city. The region is surrounded by the scenic Ashtamudi Lake and lies near the coast of the Indian Ocean, making it a unique blend of land and water ecosystems.

About the Panchayath Information System

This portal is your digital guide to the Thekkumbhagom Grama Panchayath. We have developed this comprehensive information system to promote transparency and enable data-driven planning for our community.

Here, you can explore interactive maps that display the core components of our Panchayath, including:

  • Public Services: Schools and Hospitals
  • Our Community: Population distribution
  • Economy & Recreation: Tourism and leisure spots
  • Our Land: Slope, relief, and elevation (DEM)
  • Boundaries: Administrative boundaries of our Panchayath
